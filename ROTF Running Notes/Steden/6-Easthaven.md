## In het kort
**Friendliness** ❄❄**Services** ❄❄❄**Comfort** ❄❄❄

***Available Quest.*** "[Toil and Trouble](https://5e.tools/adventure.html#idrotf,7,toil%20and%20trouble,0)".

***Population.*** 750.

***Leaders.*** Speaker Danneth Waylen (chaotic good human [commoner](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#commoner_mm)), respected for his humility and forthrightness. Captain Imdra Arlaggath (lawful good half-elf [veteran](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#veteran_mm)) commands the town's militia.

***Militia.*** Easthaven can muster up to 150 soldiers (use the [tribal warrior](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#tribal%20warrior_mm) stat block) and 12 [veterans](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#veteran_mm).

***Heraldry.*** A snowflake at the top center of a steel-gray field, above a horizontal brown field that forms the left-hand base, and a dark blue field that forms the right-hand base; where they meet is a thin white tower. The brown field represents the Eastway, the tower represents the town, and the blue field is Lac Dinneshere.

***Sacrifice to Auril.*** Humanoid (Check [[Samenvattingen#^327c0c|Sacrifices]] voor alle steden).

***Rivals.*** Caer-Dineval, Caer-Konig.
