## In het kort
**Friendliness** ❄❄❄**Services** ❄❄**Comfort** ❄❄

***Available Quest.*** "[[The White Moose]]".

***Population.*** 100.

***Leader.*** Speaker Nimsy Huddle (lawful good strongheart halfling [commoner](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#commoner_mm)) speaks for the townsfolk. Ever since the local inn closed its doors, Nimsy has opened her heated attic to visitors looking for a place to crash for the night. She bakes halfling-shaped cookies and brings them to meetings of the Council of Speakers.

***Militia.*** Lonelywood can muster up to 50 soldiers (use the [tribal warrior](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#tribal%20warrior_mm) stat block) and 4 [veterans](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#veteran_mm).

***Heraldry.*** Two narrow white triangles, one descending from the center top, the other rising from center bottom, almost meet on a forest-green field. The top triangle represents an icicle, denoting Lonelywood's northerly location; the bottom triangle is an ivory horn, representing the town's scrimshaw trade.

***Sacrifice to Auril.*** Food (Check [[Samenvattingen#^327c0c|Sacrifices]] voor alle steden).

***Rivals.*** Bremen, Targos, Termalaine.
