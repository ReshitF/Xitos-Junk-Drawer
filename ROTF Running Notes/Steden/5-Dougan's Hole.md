## In het kort
**Friendliness** ❄**Services** ❄**Comfort** ❄

***Available Quest.*** "[[Holed up]]".

***Population.*** 50.

***Leader.*** Speaker Edgra Durmoot (neutral human [scout](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#scout_mm)), a plainspoken trapper getting on in years, speaks for the townsfolk. She seldom attends meetings of the Council of Speakers.

***Militia.*** Dougan's Hole can muster up to 12 soldiers (use the [tribal warrior](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#tribal%20warrior_mm) stat block) and 1 [veteran](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#veteran_mm).

***Heraldry.*** A gray, pillar-like monolith set against a sky-blue field above two crossed silver fish, their scaly bodies forming an X, with their heads at upper left and upper right. The monolith signifies local mystery, while the fish represent the bounty of Redwaters.

***Sacrifice to Auril.*** Warmth (Check [[Samenvattingen#^327c0c|Sacrifices]] voor alle steden).

***Rival.*** Good Mead.
